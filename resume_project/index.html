Hi! I'm Taylor

I'm an award-winning investigative journalist specializing in data-driven storytelling. I believe information can be a powerful catalyst for change. That's why I'm passionate about making data publicly available and easy to understand.

I'm a fellow at The Howard Center for Investigative Journalism while earning my master's degree in computational and data reporting at the University of Maryland. My work focuses on using data to uncover stories about social issues, government accountability and equity in education.

When I'm not analyzing data or scraping government websites, you can probably find me at trivia or watching Buffy the Vampire Slayer.
